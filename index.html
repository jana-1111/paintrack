🗣️ The Speak-Up Method

1. Pause and take 3 deep breaths.

2. Lift your head. Sit tall.

3. Say aloud (or in your mind):

“I feel pain at level ?. I see you.”

4. Say: “I don’t welcome you, but I acknowledge you.”

5. Ask: “I invite calm. May this pain soften, may it pass.”

6. Breathe out slowly. Release.
